           Topic:- Coffee Shop App

6. The Target User :-
The target user would likely be coffee shop customers who are looking for a quick and convenient way to order and pick up their coffee. This app would be particularly useful for people who are in a hurry or who want to avoid waiting in long lines.
The app would also be beneficial for coffee shops, as it would allow them to streamline their ordering process and reduce wait times for customers. By using the app, coffee shops would be able to serve more customers in less time, leading to increased customer satisfaction and potentially higher sales.
Overall, the target users for this app would be coffee shop customers who value convenience and efficiency, and coffee shop owners who want to improve their operations and provide a better experience for their customers.
